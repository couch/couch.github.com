Bill Couch is a product engineer, designer, and photographer in San Francisco, California. He builds the web client of Periscope at Twitter. A fascination with storytelling and its potential with technology is what drives his long-standing obsession to create. His work history can be found on his résumé.

He lets his mind speak on Twitter, broadcasts live on Periscope, is occasionally sighted on Snapchat, observes through Instagram and Flickr, listens on Spotify, and logs it with last.fm, posts short videos to Vimeo, notes ephemera and chronicles adventures on Tumblr, and notes the internet on Pinboard.

Born in Colorado, raised in Michigan, he's smitten with the West Coast, shares a kinship with his bike, reveres the Third Coast, and treasures good conversation.

So, say hello.
